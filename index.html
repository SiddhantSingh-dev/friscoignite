What is Frisco Ignite?

Frisco Ignite is an associated chapter of IgniteSTEAM (http://www.ignitetalks.io/). The Frisco Ignite STEAM Fair is a force for building connections in Frisco among students and the surrounding communities. Ignite is about celebrating Frisco's growth in developing the creative minds of students with its inclusive appeal. The Frisco Ignite STEAM Fair is aimed at students from grades K-12. They are all welcome to listen to a team of high school speakers presenting diverse, innovative ideas within STEAM.

Ignite Fair is an event where great minds and innovative ideas intersectto inspire the next generation of students.
